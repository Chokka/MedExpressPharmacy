MedExpress Pharmacy

MedExpress was originally founded in 1995 by the National Association of People with AIDS (NAPWA), the national voice for everyone affected by HIV and AIDS. We now serve patients facing many other chronic issues in their lives such as diabetes, fertility, transplant, oncology treatments as well as senior citizens who often face hundreds of dollars each month in medication costs.

Compliance - Take your meds!

Compliance: It is reported that 30-40% of prescribed medications are never taken. A failure to comply with a medication regimen often results in the medication not working. Recognizing the critical need for our patients to take their medications, we call each member each month to make sure they have been taking their medications and to confirm that a refill is due.

Personalized Member Service

MedExpress provides personalized attention at each step along the way, improving your health by:

  • Helping you set up your MedExpress enrollment
  • Calling you each month to remind you that your refill is due
  • Working with your doctor
  • Talking to your insurance company

Questions & Answers

  1. Does it cost anything to shop with MedExpress?
    NO. Just fill out the the MedExpress enrollment forms on this website or print them out and mail them to us.

  2. Are all medications I need available from MedExpress?
    Yes. All FDA-approved medications are available through MedExpress.

  3. Does MedExpress guarantee my privacy?
    Yes. Absolutely. We know that confidentiality is critical to you. There is no reference to any medication or disease. Your name, address, and prescription information are never made available to anyone.

  4. Is MedExpress approved by my insurance company?
    Probably. Almost all insurance providers will allow you to use MedExpress.

  5. If I join MedExpress, will I have to do a lot of paperwork?
    No. Just fill out the MedExpress enrollment forms on this web site.

  6. Does MedExpress do all the paperwork with my insurance company?
    Yes. You should not have to fill out any forms for your insurance company.

  7. How do I contact MedExpress?
    We have a toll-free telephone number (1-800-MedExpress), a toll-free fax number (800-808-6331), email info@1800medexpress.com and we have a location you can visit if you wish.

  8. How does my doctor get my prescription to MedExpress? Your doctor can fax it to us on our toll-free fax line (800-808-6331) or mail it to us. When your prescription runs out, we will call your doctor and remind him that you need a refill so that there will be no interruptions in your medication regimen.

Other Services

Confidential Personalized Service

We pledge confidentiality because we know it is critical to you. To insure your confidentiality:

  • There is no reference to any medication or illness on your packaging.
  • The packaging will contain no advertising messages.
  • Your name, address, and prescription information are never made available to anyone.
  • We never sell or rent any patient information.
  • We only discuss information about you with your doctor or your insurance company.
CaseManagers & Doctors

MedExpress: More than just a pharmacy! We do more than just mail medications to our patients. We are participants in the healthcare management process working with our patient's case managers and doctors. Adherence is critical to healthcare management!

  • MedExpress calls each person every month to make sure that all refills are sent on time and to talk to our patient about any questions they may have.
  • MedExpress calls the doctors when refills are expiring so that:
  • Medications will not be interrupted.
  • We are immediately aware of any changes in medication regimens.
  • If necessary, we can serve as the liaison between the doctor and our patients, often reminding them that they need to visit their doctor.
  • MedExpress does ALL of the insurance paperwork, relieving both the patients and their case managers of this daunting activity.
  • If a case manager needs regular communication from MedExpress, we will provide it.

All that we do achieves multiple goals:

  • Higher adherence levels for our patients.
  • Less work for case managers.

MedExpress Pharmacy has a proactive approach regarding the medication process and our services have been recognized for their quality by several case management resources across the country. The bottom line is: We don’t help just our patients; We help case managers by relieving them of some of their ever increasing work load! Contact us to find out how we can help you and your patients!
